Method notes

The page uses Australian resident income-tax brackets plus the selected Medicare levy and MLS override. Capital works / depreciation is treated as a non-cash tax deduction: it reduces taxable income but does not reduce the cash-flow figure. This tool does not attempt to model every Australian edge case, including HELP, carry-forward losses, tax offsets, Division 293, land tax, trust structures, or CGT outcomes.
