Calculation notes

This estimator applies Australian resident tax brackets for 2025–26, adds the Medicare levy, subtracts configured deductions from taxable income, and optionally applies a Medicare Levy Surcharge override.

Rental cash flow is modeled separately from taxable income effects so you can see the real annual position as assumptions move. Confirm details such as ownership percentages, QS schedules, and deductible expense treatment with your accountant.
