Calculation notes

The Australian side applies resident tax brackets for 2025–26, the Medicare levy, and an optional Medicare Levy Surcharge override. The U.S. side applies 2026 federal single-filer brackets, the standard deduction if enabled, Arizona's flat individual income tax rate, and employee payroll taxes if enabled.

This is an explanatory comparison, not tax advice. It intentionally normalizes some assumptions so you can compare the shape of the two systems. Real-world U.S. rental-loss limitations, itemized deductions, health-insurance arrangements, retirement contributions, and filing choices can move the Arizona line materially.
