Calculation notes

Australia uses resident 2025–26 tax brackets, the Medicare levy, and an optional Medicare Levy Surcharge override. U.S. comparisons use 2026 federal single-filer brackets, the 2026 standard deduction, and employee payroll taxes when enabled. State layers are Arizona flat tax, Georgia flat tax, Minnesota 2026 single brackets, and modeled California and New York single-filer state tax curves.

Canada is modeled as a single filer in Ontario using 2026 federal + Ontario tax rates, basic personal tax credits, and CPP/EI when enabled. To keep the model readable, this version does not separately model Ontario surtax or the Ontario Health Premium, so the Canada line is best read as an explanatory approximation rather than a filing-accurate result.

Rental deductions are normalized across countries for comparability. Real-world U.S. passive-loss limits, Canadian rental rules, filing status choices, offsets, and itemized deductions can all move the non-Australian curves materially.
