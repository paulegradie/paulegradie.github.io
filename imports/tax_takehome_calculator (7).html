Method notes

FX mode converts with approximate market exchange rates. PPP mode converts countries with country-level purchasing-power factors and U.S. states with state regional price parity. Taxes are modeled as explanatory approximations for a single filer or resident and do not attempt to capture every credit, treaty, surcharge, payroll quirk, or passive-loss limitation.
