Method notes

FX mode uses approximate market exchange rates and PPP mode uses simplified purchasing-power or regional-price factors. Taxes remain explanatory approximations for a single filer or resident and still do not cover every credit, treaty, payroll cap edge case, or passive-loss rule. Version 8 also fixes the property cash-flow treatment so rental gains and losses affect both taxable income and net take-home instead of only altering the tax bill.
