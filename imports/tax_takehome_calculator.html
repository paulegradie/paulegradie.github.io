Calculation notes

This estimator applies Australian resident tax brackets for 2025–26, adds the Medicare levy, subtracts configured deductions from taxable income, and optionally applies a Medicare Levy Surcharge override.

It is designed for exploration and content publishing, not for lodging a return. Confirm assumptions with your accountant before relying on it.
